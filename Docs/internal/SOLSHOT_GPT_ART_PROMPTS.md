## TIPS FOR BEST RESULTS

1. **Regenerate freely** — GPT-4o image gen is included in Plus. First result is rarely perfect. Generate 3-4 and pick the best.
2. **Be specific about what you DON'T want** — "No gradients, no 3D, no text, no background" prevents common issues.
3. **Batch similar items** — Do all weapon icons in one session so they have consistent style.
4. **Clean up in Figma** — Remove backgrounds, crop to consistent sizes, ensure actual transparency.
5. **Test at target size** — Shrink every icon to 40x40 in a preview. If you can't tell what it is, simplify the prompt.
6. **Save originals** — Keep the full-res versions. You can always downscale later.
