Use the bone world positions already read into `bonePositions` in spawnRagdoll: a segment's length ≈ distance between its bone and its child bone (e.g. thigh length ≈ |Thigh.L → Shin.L|, shin ≈ |Shin.L → Foot.L|, upperArm ≈ |UpperArm.R → ForeArm.R|, foreArm ≈ |ForeArm.R → Hand.R|, torso ≈ |Root → Chest| and |Chest → Head|).
