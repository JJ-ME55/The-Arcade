  <what-built>
Complete Phase 4 combat system with:
- Hitscan shooting with hitbox-based hit detection (5 zones: head, chest, stomach, arms, legs)
- AK-47 and M4A1 rifles with distinct 30-shot spray patterns
- Pistol with faster fire rate and better moving accuracy
- Knife with left-click (40 dmg) and right-click backstab (instant kill from behind)
- CS:S damage model (base * multiplier * armor)
- Movement-based accuracy (inaccurate while moving, perfect when stopped)
- Blood particles, environment sparks, bullet decals, bullet tracers
- Headshot helmet spark effect
- Weapon switching with draw animations and weapon-specific movement speeds
- Tagging on hit (brief speed reduction)
- Ragdoll on kill
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
Launch the game and test each success criterion:

**1. Damage verification (COMBAT-01, COMBAT-04):**
- Enable debug HUD (backtick key)
- Stand still, aim at red mannequin HEAD, fire AK-47 single shot
- Expected: ~144 damage if no helmet, ~72 with helmet (check debug log)
- Fire AK-47 at BODY: expected ~18 with armor (36 * 1.0 * 0.5)
- Switch to pistol (2 key), fire at head: verify ~2 headshots to kill
- Press Y to respawn mannequin

**2. Recoil and accuracy (COMBAT-02, COMBAT-03):**
- Stand still, spray full AK-47 magazine at wall — observe recoil climbing upward then pulling left
- Switch to M4A1 (press 1 again), spray — observe tighter pattern drifting right
- Run sideways while firing — bullets should miss wildly
- Stop moving (counter-strafe) — accuracy should restore immediately
- Crouch and spray — should be tighter than standing

**3. Weapon switching (COMBAT-06):**
- Press 1, 2, 3 rapidly — each weapon has draw animation before firing is possible
- Rifle draw: ~0.7s delay, Pistol: ~0.5s, Knife: ~0.25s (fastest)
- Movement speed visibly faster with knife than rifle

**4. Visual feedback (COMBAT-10, COMBAT-11):**
- Shoot mannequin: blood particles spray from impact point
- Headshot: additional bright helmet spark flash
- Miss (hit wall): orange sparks bounce off surface, bullet hole decal appears
- Sustained fire: bullet tracers visible every ~4th shot
- Missing mannequin at range: tracer streak visible

**5. Hitbox feel (COMBAT-09):**
- Aim slightly outside mannequin head (just barely off) — should still register (generous hitbox)
- Aim at arm in front of chest — should deal chest damage (arm penetration)
- Overall: shots that LOOK like they should hit DO hit

**6. Knife and tagging (COMBAT-05, COMBAT-08):**
- Equip knife (3 key), left-click mannequin: 40 damage
- Walk behind mannequin, right-click: instant kill (backstab)
- Shoot mannequin with rifle — observe brief speed indicator in debug (tagging applied)

Report any issues with feel, values, or bugs.
