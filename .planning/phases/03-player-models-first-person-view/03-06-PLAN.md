<task type="checkpoint:human-verify" gate="blocking">
  <what-built>
  Complete Phase 3 player model and first-person view system:
  1. Third-person mannequin made of geometric primitives with 22-bone armature
  2. Procedural animations for run, strafe, crouch, jump, idle
  3. Red and blue team color variants
  4. First-person arms + weapon with two-pass rendering (no wall clipping)
  5. Gun bob synced to movement, recoil on click, muzzle flash
  6. Weapon switching (1/2/3 keys)
  7. Ragdoll physics on death (R key triggers on test mannequin)
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
  Start the server and open the game:
  ```
  npx http-server visual -p 9090
  ```
  Open http://localhost:9090 in Chrome. Click to lock pointer.

  **Check 1: Third-person mannequins**
  - Walk toward the red spawn area -- a red mannequin should be visible nearby, walking in place with animated arms and legs
  - Walk toward the blue spawn area -- a blue mannequin should be visible, standing idle
  - Both mannequins should be clearly distinguishable by color (solid red vs solid blue)
  - Mannequins should be roughly human-proportioned (~1.8m tall, arms and legs in right places)

  **Check 2: Procedural animations**
  - The red mannequin should have visible arm swing and leg stride (walk cycle)
  - Movement should look natural (arms opposite to legs, body bob)

  **Check 3: First-person view**
  - You should see arms + rifle in the bottom-right of your view
  - Walk around: gun should bob (sway side to side, bounce up and down) in rhythm with movement
  - Stop moving: bob should settle to center

  **Check 4: Recoil and muzzle flash**
  - Left-click: rifle should kick back (recoil) and spring forward
  - Brief yellow/orange flash at muzzle on click
  - Multiple rapid clicks should show repeated kicks

  **Check 5: Weapon switching**
  - Press 1: rifle (long weapon)
  - Press 2: pistol (short, compact)
  - Press 3: knife (blade)
  - Each should look visually distinct

  **Check 6: Wall clipping**
  - Walk up to a wall with the weapon visible
  - The weapon should NOT clip through or disappear behind the wall
  - It should stay fully visible overlaid on top

  **Check 7: Ragdoll**
  - Navigate near the red mannequin and press R
  - The animated mannequin should disappear and be replaced by falling body parts
  - Parts should collapse under gravity, connected at joints (not flying apart)
  - Should settle on the ground within 2-3 seconds
  - Should disappear after ~5 seconds

  **Check 8: No regressions**
  - WASD movement, jumping, crouching all still work
  - Camera responds to mouse
  - No FPS drops (check status display for speed/ground info)
  </how-to-verify>
  <resume-signal>Type "approved" if all checks pass, or describe specific issues (e.g., "ragdoll body parts fly apart", "no muzzle flash visible", "FP weapon clips into walls")</resume-signal>
